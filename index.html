    Meet Our Executive Team

    A motivated group of teenagers all passionate about sharing technology with the world!

    Lola Cantillon

    Lola Cantillon, a student at Newark Academy, ​​is a charismatic and driven student, and a researcher with a passion for creative writing, and social marketing. Lola is a social entrepreneur looking to improve the lives of others. She is incredibly motivated and driven to make a difference in her community.

    Juliet Bu

    Juliet Bu is a senior at Newark Academy and a graduate from The School for Ethics and Global Leadership. As a member of her school’s Equity and Inclusion Team and the president of the Anatomy and Entrepreneurship Clubs, Juliet believes wholeheartedly in the power of non-profit organizations to change communities and break down educational inequities, especially in STEM.

    Nitya Gupta

    Nitya Gupta is a rising senior at Newark Academy, serving as a director for Techshare this year. Nitya is a social and climate justice activist and a leader within her student and local community. She joined Techshare because she wanted to get involved with an organization that brought opportunities, girls, and minorities in STEM.
